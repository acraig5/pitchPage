About Me

Hello, my name is Alexander "Alex" Craig. I grew up on the Yakama reservation and strive to be an inspiration for the younger generation of my community that it is possible to get off the reservation and see the rest of the world. From 2014-2016, I attended Eastern Washington University obtaining my BA in Visual Communication Design

I currently work at Yakima Valley Farm Workers Clinic as an Youth Development Instrutor where I teach Outdoor Adventure where I create lesson plans for middle school to early high school students camping and survial skills when in the outdoors
